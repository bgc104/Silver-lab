Featured Development: Gem Chase

Gem Chase provides a structured look at the sports card market for collectors who buy raw and sell graded. By processing price gaps, gem rates, and seasonal market trends, the app identifies cards with significant spreads between raw and PSA-graded values. Our goal is to replace "gut feel" with a systematic approach, using an algorithm that weighs liquidity and market timing to help you organize your next grading submission.
